Mignonne Gavigan

Work:
Website, E­commerce, Photography, Graphic Design / E ­ Book, Email Marketing, Web Design, PhotoShop

In 2009, Mignonne Gavigan created her original scarf necklace, the Le Charlot, under her Mignonne Gavigan label. She took this piece to a sales meeting with a Bergdorf Goodman buyer who asked to join Mignonne to help with the sales and marketing effort. Mignonne agreed to add her to the team and the two formed Roarke New York, LLC and began selling the scarf necklaces under the Roarke label. While Mignonne is very proud of the work she created while with Roarke New York, LLC, she is very excited to launch a new company that will sell her designs (both old and new) under her original Mignonne Gavigan brand name. With a fresh new vision and the breadth of experience she has gained over her decade in fashion, Mignonne will continue to create stunning pieces that enhance even the most collected closet.
